NC-News

NC-News is a social news aggregation, web content rating and discussion website. It features articles which are divided into topics, and each article has user-curated ratings from upvotes and downvotes using its back-end API. Users can also add comments about an article and toggle dark-mode on and off.

Ekans

Welcome to Ekans, a unique twist on the classic Snake game. Developed using Godot and GDScript, Ekans reverses the traditional mechanics of Snake by starting the player with a fully grown snake that shrinks as it eats food. As the snake shrinks, it drops obstacles that clutter the map, creating deadly traps that increase the difficulty.

Features of Ekans:

  • Reverse Snake Dynamics - Starts with a fully grown snake that shrinks when eating food, reversing traditional Snake mechanics for a unique gameplay experience.
  • Dynamic Hazard Generation - Shrinking snake leaves behind hazardous obstacles, adding strategic depth and increasing difficulty as the game progresses.
  • Intuitive and Responsive Controls - Smooth and responsive controls ensure seamless gameplay and easy navigation, appealing to a wide range of players.
  • Escalating Difficulty - Game complexity increases as obstacles clutter the play area, maintaining player engagement with new challenges.
  • Mobile Optimization - Optimized for mobile performance, providing a smooth gaming experience on the go with a dedicated mobile version.
  • Secure Firebase Authentication - Uses Firebase for robust user authentication, leveraging GDScript for secure and efficient user management.
